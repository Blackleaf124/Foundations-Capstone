Weapons

Revolvers

Light-weight handguns that are nimble and easy to aim. Their lack of stopping power is made up for by their fast fire rate and quick reload.

Lever-Action

Simple and reliable rifles that bring a good balance of stoping power and mobility allowing for hunters to stay relatively mobile and still land devastating blows.

Double-Barrel Shotgun

Large, powerful hand cannons that hit with incredible force. Good at dealing a lot of damage with each shot, but only having room for two shots at a time makes handling them rather slow.

Ammo

Silver-Bullets

Bullets made of church ordained silver. The metal is a powerful deterrent against unholy beings.

Toxic Rounds

Special rounds designed to deliver powerful toxins to their targets bloodstream and respritory system. The rounds explode on impact filling the air with poison as well as their wounds.

Napalm Rounds

High-tech rounds developed to explode with flaming oil on impact. Anything weak to fire is easily suppressed by a few rounds of these.

Zeus's Tears

Strange copper bullets that are loaded with electricity. On impact this energy is discharged. Most effective against monsters susceptible to being stunned

Explosive Rounds

These bullets trade direct penetration power for an ear splitting, concussive blast on impact.

Azure Comets

These rounds are packed with a brain numbing compound that fills the air around their victim. The effect acts quickly and is very effective and neutralizing psychic monsters.

Gear

Mental Resolve Serum

A powerful elixer used to grant hunters resistance to mental attacks and protect them from the effect of Azure Comet bullets.

Poison Gas Grenades

Simple hand-held bombs that are loaded with powerful toxic gas. Each satchel of grenades comes with a gas mask to protect the hunter.

Flash Bombs

Small, round bombs that when thrown at the ground explode in a blinding flash of light. Great against monsters susceptible to being stunned.

Molotov Cocktails

A little barbaric and makeshift in design, but stunningly effective at starting fires. Used commonly against fire-hating beasts.

Golden Crucifix

These simple looking keepsakes are generous gifts from the church to our guilds' hunters. They are blessed by the arch bishop and forged out of pure gold. They miraculously fill demonic entities with desperate fear.

Sound Bombs

These unique tools inspired the popular 'Explosive Rounds' These are more powerful versions that are thrown by hand instead of fired. The explosion releases a concussive blast that leaves certain monsters shaken and weak.
